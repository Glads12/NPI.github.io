Nkomazi Prominent Innovators


Empowering youth through innovation


Nkomazi Prominent Innovators (NPI) Project Is a non-profit service delivery initiative established in 2023, located in Mangweni (temporary), with the aim of aiding high school learners, institutions (hospitals and clinics), traditional authorities (libraries, community halls, youth centers and charity centers), local government, and community at large with different skills and services. Computer laboratories, infrastructure development, career guidance, the fourth industrial revolution, entrepreneurship etc. Are the services with paramount importance to be rendered by the company to help alleviate unemployment level and combat crime.

There is a real need for our community located remote areas to be exposed to digital skills.
